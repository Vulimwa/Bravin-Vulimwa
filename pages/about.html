MAIN BIO

I'm Bravin a Data Scientist and Urban & Regional Planner in Kenya who enjoys transforming complex data into meaningful insights that drive better decisions .

I spend my time exploring data, building machine learning models, developing interactive dashboards, and applying GIS to solve real-world challenges. Whether it's analyzing business performance, supporting spatial planning, or uncovering patterns hidden in data, my goal is to create solutions that are practical, reliable, and impactful for the people who depend on them.

Through academic projects, professional certifications, and continuous learning, I've worked with diverse datasets across business, environmental, and urban planning domains. From cleaning and modeling data to designing business intelligence dashboards and spatial decision-support tools, I've developed solutions that combine analytical thinking with real-world problem-solving.

Currently, I'm advancing my expertise through the ALX Data Science program while building a portfolio of projects in machine learning, business intelligence, GIS, and data analytics. My background in Urban & Regional Planning gives me a unique perspective on understanding complex systems and using data to support sustainable development and informed decision-making.
When I'm not working with data, you'll usually find me exploring new analytical techniques, learning emerging technologies, or thinking about how data can be used to solve the next meaningful challenge.

HOW I WORK

Starting with the question, not the data

Before analyzing a dataset, I want to understand the problem we're trying to solve. Good analysis begins with asking the right questions, identifying the real objective, and choosing the most meaningful approach. The best insights come from understanding the context before opening the data.

ask first, analyze second.

Turning data into decisions

Data is only valuable when it leads to action. I focus on transforming raw, messy information into clear insights through analytics, machine learning, GIS, and visualization, helping organizations make confident, evidence-based decisions.

insights over information.

Collaboration drives better outcomes

Great solutions come from working with people who understand the problem from different perspectives. I enjoy collaborating with stakeholders, domain experts, and teams to build solutions that are technically sound, practical, and aligned with real-world needs.

better together.

Details create confidence

Reliable data products are built on careful attention to detail—from data quality and cleaning to model validation and dashboard design. Small details improve accuracy, build trust, and ensure the insights people rely on are dependable.

quality builds trust.
