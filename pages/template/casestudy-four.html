The Real Problem

Migori Town has experienced rapid and uncoordinated urban growth, resulting in land-use conflicts, infrastructure deficits, traffic congestion, environmental degradation, and unequal access to public services. Without a comprehensive spatial development framework, sustainable growth and investment become increasingly difficult.

The Approach

Develop a 10-year Local Physical and Land Use Development Plan by combining spatial analysis, GIS mapping, infrastructure assessment, field research, and planning principles to produce evidence-based development proposals.

Working as part of a multidisciplinary planning team, the project translated complex spatial and socio-economic data into actionable planning strategies that support coordinated urban development.

What I Built

  1. Produced the project basemap and supporting GIS datasets using ArcGIS Pro
  2. Conducted physical and social infrastructure analysis to identify service gaps and development needs
  3. Contributed to technical report writing and documentation of planning findings
  4. Developed and digitized the proposed land use plan based on planning objectives and spatial analysis
  5. Assisted in preparing presentation materials and visual communication for the final planning report

What Changed

  • Produced a comprehensive spatial planning framework to guide Migori's development between 2025 and 2035
  • Supported evidence-based land use allocation and infrastructure planning
  • Identified key development constraints and opportunities through GIS and spatial analysis
  • Contributed to a planning document that promotes sustainable urban growth, improved service delivery, and coordinated future development
